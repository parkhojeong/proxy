*BeanPostProcessor*
- postProcessBeforeInitialization: after instance creation, before initialization like @PostConstruct
- postProcessAfterInitialization: after instance creation, after initialization like @PostConstruct

* @PostConstruct
- @PostConstruct annotation is just initialization after bean creation.
  - that is, bean post processor handle @PostConstruct annotation.
- spring auto register CommonAnnotationBeanPostProcessor.   
  - and CommonAnnotationBeanPostProcessor invoke @PostConstruct method
